TEMPLEOS FOR MACOS
==================

1. Drag TempleOS.app into the Applications folder.

2. First launch: RIGHT-CLICK the app and choose "Open" — it is unsigned.
   On newer macOS you may also need System Settings > Privacy & Security
   and click "Open Anyway".

3. The app takes it from there: it checks for QEMU (offering to install
   it via Homebrew if missing), creates the virtual machine, and walks
   you through TempleOS's own installer. Every launch after that boots
   straight into the temple.

The VM lives in ~/Library/Application Support/TempleOS.
